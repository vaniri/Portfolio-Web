FitMeUp

FitMeUp is a social networking web app focused on fitness and healthy lifestyle. Share your progress and fitness tips with others to help them stay fit and motivated! Users can create posts, leave comments and reactions to posts they like or find insightful.

feeds-react

Stay tuned and read all your favorite news in one place with the rss-feeds web app! Users can add their favorite RSS feeds (which are updated automatically), read them and even leave comments.

wavMe

Filling a profile does not define you, music does! Add music you like and the wavMe web app finds users matching your perfect taste. Users can create account using the Spotify or Last.FM websites. Users tastes similarity is calculated using javascript sets intersection method.
